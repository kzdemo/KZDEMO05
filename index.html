"Litigation is not a commodity. Neither are we."

Koning Zollar LLP is a boutique litigation firm built on a simple premise: sophisticated clients deserve sophisticated counsel. We bring the experience of major international firms with the attention and efficiency only a focused practice can deliver.

Our attorneys have litigated on behalf of Fortune 500 corporations, emerging companies, and individuals alike — in federal and state courts across the country. From San Diego to New Haven, we handle matters that require both intellectual rigor and relentless advocacy.
